**Goal:** Create a landing page for Brother's Live Cartoon booking service with business information display and direct contact options via phone and WhatsApp.

- Display business information including company name, tagline, address, and contact phone number
- Show list of available cartoon characters (Gorilla, Red Teddy, Yellow Teddy, Doraemon, Mickey Mouse, Motu-Patlu) with images
- List event types supported (Birthday Party, Wedding Entry, other Events)
- Highlight service features (High Quality Costumes, Energetic Performers, Reasonable Rates)
- Add "Call Now" button that opens phone dialer with 9427065911 prefilled
- Add "Book on WhatsApp" button that opens WhatsApp with prefilled message to 919427065911
- Apply vibrant, playful visual theme with orange as primary brand color

**User-visible outcome:** Users can view business information, see available cartoon characters with images, and quickly contact the service via phone call or WhatsApp booking.
